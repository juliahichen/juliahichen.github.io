SB2 Enterprises

UI Design, HTML/CSS

My client, a tech consultant, wanted an extremely simple online business card to allow prospective clients contact him easily. He wanted to show that he's professional yet fun and wanted a website that looked nice on computer screens as well as mobile browsers.

Wireframe

The target audience of this website was SB2 Enterprises’ prospective clients so I made a few sketches that utilized the above the fold space so that prospective clients could focus on what’s important: getting in touch.

Visual Design

After the client approved the layout, I used a color scheme that showed consistency with SB2 Enterprises' brand image. I presented the client with multiple variations of the color scheme. The ones below are just a few examples.

The client wanted to seem trustworthy and experienced but still fun, so in the end I decided to use a dark blue background with a touch of pink for the button.

Code

I coded SB2's business card in HTML and CSS. I wrote a bunch of CSS media queries to ensure that the site looked good on mobile devices and added a reset stylesheet to make it consistent cross-browser.
